Our history

My journey began in 2009 when it became clear that my oldest child had many, many allergies to both skincare products and food. Finding soap--and especially, lip balm--he could use was extremely difficult. I searched and searched for a company that offered an allergen free product and production line and just could not find it. I started making soap and lip balm just to have something safe to use on my children.

One day, I realized many others were in the same boat, so Allergy Mama was born.

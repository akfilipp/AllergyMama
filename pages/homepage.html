Our history

My journey began in 2009 when it became clear that my oldest child had many, many allergies to both skin products and food. Finding soap and especially lip balm he could use was extremely difficult. I searched and searched for a company that offered an allergen free product and production line and just could not find it. I started making soap and lip balm just to have something safe to use on my children. One day, I realized many others were in the same boat, so Allergy Mama was born.

My vision and promise to all people, parents and my children, is that I will make a product that is allergen, gluten and fragrance free every time. All equipment was purchased new and is dedicated to production. I want you to feel safe with the product that I make.

If you ever have any questions, please email me at AllergyMamaSoap@gmail.com.

My products will always be free of the following:

  • Casein (cow and goat)
  • Gluten
  • Peanuts
  • Tree Nuts*
  • Shellfish
  • Soy
  • Eggs
  • Corn
  • Sesame
  • Sunflower
  • Garlic
  • Latex
  • Lanolin
  • Nickel
  • Cobalt
  • Formaldehyde
  • Methylchloroisothiazolinone
  • Disodium EDTA
  • Methyliothiazolinone
  • Synthetic Fragrance
  • Lavender

    • *The use of coconut is based off of a statement by the American College of Allergy, Asthma & Immunology that coconut is a fruit even though the FDA categorizes it as a tree nut. www.acaai.org/allergies/types/food-allergies

    *The use of shea nut butter is based off a statement by Food Allergy Research and Resource Program at University of Nebraska-Lincoln. www.farrp.unl.edu/shea-nut-butter

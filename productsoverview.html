Our history

My journey began in 2009 when it became clear that my oldest child had many, many allergies to both skincare products and food. Finding soap--and especially, lip balm--he could use was extremely difficult. I searched and searched for a company that offered an allergen free product and production line and just could not find it. I started making soap and lip balm just to have something safe to use on my children.

One day, I realized many others were in the same boat, so Allergy Mama was born.

My vision and promise to all people, parents and my children: I will make a product that is allergen-free every time. All equipment was purchased new and is dedicated to production. I want you to feel safe with everything you purchase from me.

My products will always be free of the following:

  • Casein (cow and goat)
  • Gluten
  • Peanuts
  • Tree Nuts*
  • Shellfish
  • Soy
  • Eggs
  • Corn
  • Sesame
  • Sunflower
  • Garlic
  • Disodium EDTA
  • Latex
  • Lanolin
  • Nickel
  • Cobalt
  • Formaldehyde
  • Methylchloroisothiazolinone
  • Methyliothiazolinone
  • Synthetic Fragrance
  • Lavender
    • *The use of coconut is based off of a statement by the American College of Allergy, Asthma & Immunology that coconut is a fruit even though the FDA categorizes it as a tree nut. www.acaai.org/allergies/types/food-allergies

    *The use of shea nut butter is based off a statement by Food Allergy Research and Resource Program at University of Nebraska-Lincoln. www.farrp.unl.edu/shea-nut-butter
